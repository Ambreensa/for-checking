How to Make Hyderabadi Biryani
    1.Clean the meat. Now in a pan, add meat, salt, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, green chilli paste, sauteed brown onions, cardamom powder, cinnamon, cumin seeds, cloves, mace, mint leaves and lemon juice.2.Mix all of these ingredients thoroughly together.3.Add curd, clarified butter, semi-cooked rice, saffron, water and oil. Mix it well.4.Now apply sticky dough on the sides of the pan.
